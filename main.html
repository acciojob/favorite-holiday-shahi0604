Paris, France


Paris, known as the "City of Light," is a world-renowned destination famous for its stunning architecture, rich history, and vibrant art scene. Located along the Seine River, it offers a romantic atmosphere and some of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The Eiffel Tower – A global cultural icon of France.
  2. The Louvre Museum – The world's largest art museum.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. The Arc de Triomphe – Standing at the western end of the Champs-Élysées.
  5. Disneyland Paris – A magical theme park for all ages.

Must-Try Foods

  • Freshly baked Croissants – Buttery and flaky pastry.
  • Delicious Macarons – Colorful and sweet meringue-based confections.
  • Escargot – A traditional French dish of cooked land snails.
  • Classic Ratatouille – A healthy and flavorful vegetable stew.
  • Creamy Crème Brûlée – A rich custard base topped with hardened caramelized sugar.
